Privacy Policy

for Stitch Infinite

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

Quick Summary: Stitch Infinite is a completely offline puzzle game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your game data stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Stitch Infinite! This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Stitch Infinite is developed and maintained by Hemanth Guvvala. By using this App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We do NOT collect any personal information. The App does not require, request, or collect:

  • Name or email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media files
  • Device identifiers for tracking

2.2 Game Data (Local Storage Only)

The App stores the following game-related data locally on your device only:

  • Game Statistics: Best score, total games played, total score, highest tile achieved
  • Game Progress: Current game state, tile positions, and current score
  • Gameplay Data: Total moves made, last played timestamp
  • Settings: User preferences for game settings

This data is stored using Android's Room Database and remains entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

The locally stored game data is used solely for:

  • Maintaining your game progress between sessions
  • Tracking your personal best scores and statistics
  • Providing the undo functionality during gameplay
  • Displaying your achievement history
  • Preserving your game settings and preferences

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure Room Database framework. This data is protected by Android's app sandboxing, which prevents other apps from accessing your Stitch Infinite data.

4.2 No Cloud Backup

The App does not use cloud storage or backup services. Your data remains exclusively on your device.

4.3 Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Reset Statistics: Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings to clear all game statistics
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored game data

5. Third-Party Services

Stitch Infinite does NOT integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud storage services

The App operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

6. Permissions

Stitch Infinite does NOT request any special Android permissions. The App only uses standard permissions required for basic functionality:

  • Storage: Used only to save game data locally on your device

No network, camera, microphone, or location permissions are requested or used.

7. Children's Privacy

Stitch Infinite is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, the App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Parents and guardians can be confident that their children's privacy is fully protected when using this App.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or disclose any data to third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect personal information, there is nothing to share.

We will never:

  • Sell your information to advertisers or data brokers
  • Share data with third-party services or partners
  • Transfer data across borders or to external servers
  • Use your data for marketing or profiling purposes

9. International Users

Stitch Infinite can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not transmit any information, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. Your data never leaves your device.
