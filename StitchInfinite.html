Privacy Policy

for Stitch Infinite

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

Quick Summary: Stitch Infinite is a puzzle game that operates primarily offline with optional online features. We collect minimal data locally for game functionality. Third-party services (Facebook Ads, Google Play Games) may collect data according to their policies when you use those features.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Stitch Infinite! This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Stitch Infinite is developed and maintained by Hemanth Guvvala. By using this App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information We Collect Directly

The App itself does NOT collect any personally identifiable information. We do not require, request, or collect:

  • Name or email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media files (except when you choose to share game screenshots)

2.2 Game Data (Local Storage Only)

The App stores the following game-related data locally on your device using Android Room Database:

  • Player Statistics: Best score, total games played, total score, highest tile achieved, total moves
  • Game Progress: Current game state, tile positions, current score, last played timestamp
  • In-Game Currency: Coins earned, power-up counts (bombs, shuffles, hints)
  • Achievements & Challenges: Achievement progress, completed challenges, unlocked achievements
  • Streak Data: Current streak, longest streak, last completion date, total days played
  • Theme Preferences: Selected theme, unlocked themes
  • Settings: Haptic feedback preference, sound settings, onboarding status
  • Game Modes: Selected game mode preference

This data is stored using Android's Room Database and SharedPreferences, and remains entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers.

2.3 Data Collected by Third-Party Services

When you use certain optional features, third-party services may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

Google Play Games Services (Optional)

When you sign in to Google Play Games for achievements and leaderboards, Google may collect:

  • Google Account information (email, profile)
  • Game scores and achievement data
  • Play Games activity and usage statistics
  • Device information and identifiers

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Note: You can play the entire game without signing into Google Play Games.

Facebook Audience Network (Advertising)

The App displays advertisements through Facebook Audience Network. When ads are shown, Facebook may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • App usage patterns for ad targeting
  • Network information (carrier, connection type)

Privacy Policy: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/
Ad Choices: https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences

Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)

When you make in-app purchases, Google Play handles all payment processing. Google may collect:

  • Purchase transaction details
  • Payment information (processed securely by Google)
  • Google Account information

Note: We do not have access to your payment card details. All payments are processed securely by Google Play.

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

The App's core functionality does not automatically collect analytics or usage data. However, third-party services mentioned above may automatically collect technical information such as:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address (collected by ad networks)
  • Network connection type

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Locally Stored Game Data

The locally stored game data is used solely for:

  • Maintaining your game progress between sessions
  • Tracking your personal best scores and statistics
  • Providing power-ups and in-game currency system
  • Managing achievements, challenges, and daily streaks
  • Applying your selected theme and settings
  • Displaying your achievement and leaderboard history

3.2 Third-Party Service Data Usage

Data collected by third-party services is used by those services for:

  • Google Play Games: Syncing achievements and leaderboards, providing social gaming features
  • Facebook Ads: Delivering relevant advertisements, measuring ad performance, monetizing the free app
  • Google Play Billing: Processing in-app purchases securely

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure Room Database framework and SharedPreferences. This data is protected by Android's app sandboxing, which prevents other apps from accessing your Stitch Infinite data.

4.2 No Cloud Backup by Us

We do not use cloud storage or backup services for your game data. Your data remains exclusively on your device. However, Android's automatic backup feature may back up SharedPreferences to your Google account - this is controlled by your device settings.

4.3 Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Reset Game Data: Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings to clear all game statistics and progress
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored game data
  • Third-Party Data: To delete data collected by third parties, refer to their privacy policies and opt-out mechanisms

6. Permissions

Stitch Infinite requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required for displaying ads and syncing with Google Play Games (when signed in)
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check internet connectivity before loading ads or syncing data

Storage: The app uses standard local storage permissions to save game data. No additional storage permissions are requested.

No camera, microphone, precise location, or contacts permissions are requested or used.

7. Advertising and Tracking

7.1 Advertisements

Stitch Infinite displays advertisements through Facebook Audience Network to support the free version of the app. These ads may be:

  • Banner ads (displayed during gameplay)
  • Interstitial ads (displayed between games)
  • Rewarded video ads (optional, shown when earning bonus coins)

7.2 Ad Personalization

Facebook may use your Android Advertising ID to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Facebook Ad Preferences: https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences

7.3 No Analytics Tracking

We do NOT use analytics services like Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics to track your behavior within the app. The only tracking that occurs is through the advertising network for ad delivery and measurement.

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Restrict Processing: Disable optional features like Google Play Games

  • Right to Data Portability: Since data is stored locally, you can access it through the app

11.2 California (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected (see Section 2)
  • Request deletion of personal information (reset via Settings or uninstall)
  • Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (we don't sell data; third-party services have their own opt-out mechanisms)

11.3 General Rights

All users have the right to:

  • Access their locally stored game data through the app interface
  • Delete their data by resetting in Settings or uninstalling
