Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Welcome to PDF Toolkit. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Key Point: PDF Toolkit is designed with privacy in mind. All PDF processing happens locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal files or documents to any external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Files and Documents

PDF Toolkit processes PDF files and images that you select on your device. These files are:

  • Processed locally on your device only
  • Never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers
  • Stored in your device's local storage in the Downloads/PDFToolkit folder
  • Under your complete control - you can delete them anytime

1.2 Device Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To read input files and save processed PDFs
  • Internet: For displaying ads (free version) and processing in-app purchases

We do not access any other data on your device.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics. Your app usage remains completely private.

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Facebook Audience Network (Ads)

The free version of PDF Toolkit displays ads through Facebook Audience Network. Facebook may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

Facebook's data collection is subject to their own privacy policy. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. Pro users do not see any ads.

Facebook Audience Network Privacy Policy: https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy

2.2 Google Play Billing

For in-app purchases and subscriptions, we use Google Play's billing system. Google handles all payment processing and may collect:

  • Transaction information
  • Purchase history
  • Google account information

We do not store or have access to your payment information. All transactions are processed securely by Google Play.

Google Play Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to use! However:

  • File Processing: Your files are processed locally to perform PDF operations (merge, compress, convert, split)
  • Storage: Processed files are saved to your device's Downloads/PDFToolkit folder
  • Purchases: Google Play tracks your subscription status to unlock Pro features

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your information to anyone. Your files never leave your device. The only data shared is:

  • To Facebook: Anonymous ad-related data (free users only)
  • To Google: Purchase verification data (when you buy Pro)

Both are governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Your data security is ensured by:

  • Local Processing: All operations happen on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't store files on external servers
  • Android Security: Files are protected by your device's security features
  • No Accounts: No login required, no user accounts, no passwords to secure

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 File Control

  • Delete Files: Remove any processed PDFs from Downloads/PDFToolkit folder anytime
  • Uninstall App: Removes all app data (but not your saved PDFs)

6.2 Advertising Choices

  • Opt Out of Personalized Ads: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Upgrade to Pro: Remove all ads permanently

6.3 Subscription Management

  • Cancel Anytime: Play Store → Subscriptions → PDF Toolkit Pro
  • Restore Purchases: Use "Restore Purchases" button in app settings

7. Children's Privacy

PDF Toolkit does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 18+ due to advertising content. We recommend parental supervision for younger users.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Users

PDF Toolkit is available globally. Since all processing happens locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. The only cross-border data flows are through Google Play (for purchases) and Facebook (for ads), governed by their privacy policies.

10. Legal Compliance

PDF Toolkit complies with:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California)
  • Other Privacy Laws: Applicable data protection regulations worldwide

Since we don't collect personal data, most privacy law requirements don't apply. However, we maintain transparency and user control as best practices.
